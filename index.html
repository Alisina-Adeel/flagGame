🌍 About This Game

This game is designed to help players learn and become familiar with flags from every country in the world. By playing, you will improve your memory, recognize national flags, and build your global knowledge in a fun and interactive way.

Whether you're a beginner or a geography expert, this game makes learning flags simple, engaging, and enjoyable.
